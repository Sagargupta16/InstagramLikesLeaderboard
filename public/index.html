Instagram Likes Leaderboard

Find out who likes your posts the most. See ranked leaderboards of your biggest fans — both people you follow and people you don't.

How to use

  1. Log in to instagram.com in your browser
  2. Click the "Copy Code" button below
  3. Open Developer Tools (F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I / Cmd+Option+I)
  4. Go to the Console tab
  5. Paste the code and press Enter
  6. Click RUN and wait for the scan to complete

This tool runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
Use responsibly — excessive API calls may result in temporary restrictions from Instagram.
